Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers.

What areas do you cover in Dhaka?

We are rapidly expanding! Currently, we provide full coverage in Gulshan, Banani, Dhanmondi, and Uttara. Please contact our support team with your address to check for availability in other areas as we are adding new locations every month.

Is there a data limit on your plans?

Absolutely not. All of our internet plans come with completely unlimited data. You can stream, download, and game as much as you want without any throttling or extra charges.

What is FTTH and why is it better?

FTTH stands for Fiber To The Home. It means we run a pure fiber-optic cable directly to your residence. This technology is superior to traditional cable or DSL because it offers significantly higher speeds, lower latency (less lag), and much greater reliability, as it's not susceptible to the same kinds of interference.

How long does installation take?

After confirming your order, we aim to complete the installation within 24 to 48 hours, depending on your location and building permissions. Our technical team will schedule a specific time that is convenient for you.
